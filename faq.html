Frequently Asked Questions

What is Career Hub?

Career Hub is a centralized platform designed to help students access internships, certification courses, and job opportunities. It consolidates all relevant information shared by teachers into a single, user-friendly website.

Who can use Career Hub?

Career Hub is intended for students of the college seeking internships, certification courses, and job opportunities. Teachers and administrators can also use the platform to share and manage opportunities.

How do I create an account?

To create an account, go to the "Sign Up" page and provide your email, student ID, and a password. You will receive a confirmation email to verify your account after submission.

How do I log in to my account?

Visit the "Login" page and enter your registered email and password. If you forget your password, use the "Forgot Password" link to reset it.

What should I do if I forget my password?

If you forget your password, click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You will receive instructions to reset your password via email.

How can I update my profile information?

After logging in, go to your profile page where you can update your personal information and preferences.

How do I search for opportunities?

You can use the search bar and filters on the main page to look for internships, certification courses, and job opportunities. Filter by category, location, and other criteria to find what you need.

Can I bookmark opportunities?

Yes, you can bookmark opportunities to save them for later. Click the bookmark icon next to the listing youâ€™re interested in.

How do I apply for an opportunity?

Click on the opportunity listing to view its details and follow the provided application instructions or links to apply.

How can I contact Career Hub support?

For any questions or issues, you can reach out to us through the [Contact Us](contact.html) page.

How often is the opportunity feed updated?

The opportunity feed is updated regularly to ensure that you have access to the latest internships, certification courses, and job openings.

Can teachers also use Career Hub?

Yes, teachers can use Career Hub to share and manage opportunities. They can access the admin panel to add, edit, or remove listings and manage user accounts.

How do I become an admin?

If you are a teacher or administrator and wish to become an admin, please contact us through the [Contact Us](contact.html) page to request admin access.

Is Career Hub mobile-friendly?

Yes, Career Hub is designed to be mobile-friendly and accessible on various devices, including smartphones and tablets.

How is my data protected?

Your data is protected using industry-standard security measures. We follow best practices to ensure your personal information is stored securely.

Can I suggest new features or improvements?

We welcome feedback and suggestions. Please share your ideas through the [Contact Us](contact.html) page to help us improve the platform.
